About

LudoNode provides software developer support. We focus on helping developers who need experienced guidance. Our services include mentoring, AI code cleanup, and vibe coding support.

Dave Balmer

Founder and CEO

Dave brings nearly 50 years of software development expertise to the table. He’s also a game designer, composer, graphic designer, and pixel artist.

I’m here to help and I bring decades of know-how with me. I do full-time development on my own projects, but make time to help other developers. I enjoy helping them learn new stuff or get past some hurdle they are struggling with.

Dave started programming at 6. In those days, personal computers were a new thing. Since then, he’s been at the forefront of several waves of computing technology including Enterprise Software Development, Networking and Internet Development, Web Development, Mobile App Development, and more recently AI-Assisted Development.

  • He’s been doing this for decades and have worked everywhere from tiny startups to large global brands as a consultant or full-time employee. Some notable names: HP, Yahoo, Palm, Nielsen, ARCO, AT&T, and Microsoft.

  • He’s been CEO, CTO, Staff Engineer, Senior Software Engineer, Senior Developer, Product Manager, Developer Relations Engineer, and Consultant.

  • Dave has used a lot of languages over the years including: JavaScript, Perl, Python, Prolog, Cobol, C, C++, TypeScript, CSS, Lua, BASIC, Visual Basic, Assembly, and Go. If a language isn’t on this list, there’s a good chance that I can read it and understand the important concepts behind it. Ask if you’re uncertain.

  • He’s used lots of database systems: SQL (a dozen flavors), MongoDB, Redis, and a bunch that have lost favor over the years but still had interesting ideas to contribute.

  • And all the typical tools like: Jira, Github, Miro, Slack, Zoom, Google Docs, and Discord. Basically, whatever gets the job done.
